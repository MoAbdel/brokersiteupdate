## Washington State-Specific Notes

### Property Tax Considerations
- Washington has no state income tax
- Property taxes vary by county (King County: ~1%)
- Senior/disabled property tax exemptions available

### Market Characteristics
- Tech wealth concentration (Amazon, Microsoft, Google)
- Significant appreciation in last decade
- Strong equity positions for long-term homeowners

### HECM Considerations
- No state income tax benefit for reverse mortgage
- High home values = jumbo reverse mortgage demand
- Strong aging-in-place culture

### Equity Product Considerations
- HELOC popular for renovations
- Cash-out refinance for tech professionals
- Second home financing common (San Juan Islands, ski)
