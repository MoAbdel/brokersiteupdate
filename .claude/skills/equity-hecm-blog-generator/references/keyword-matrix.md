## Keyword Usage Rules

### Primary Keyword Placement
1. **H1 Title:** Exact match required
2. **First 100 words:** Exact or close variant
3. **Meta title:** Exact match in first 60 characters
4. **Meta description:** Exact match in first 60 characters
5. **At least 2 H2s:** Exact or close variant
6. **Image alt text:** At least 1 instance
7. **URL slug:** Hyphenated version

### Secondary Keyword Integration
- Use 3-5 secondary keywords per post
- Integrate naturally in body content
- Include in H3 subheadings where appropriate
- Use in FAQ questions

### LSI/Semantic Terms

**HECM Track LSI Terms:**
- senior housing, aging in place, retirement income
- FHA-insured, HUD-approved, non-recourse
- principal limit, mortgage insurance premium
- home equity conversion, reverse equity

**Equity Track LSI Terms:**
- home equity access, equity extraction
- second lien, subordinate financing
- draw period, repayment period
- loan-to-value, combined loan-to-value
- debt-to-income, credit utilization
