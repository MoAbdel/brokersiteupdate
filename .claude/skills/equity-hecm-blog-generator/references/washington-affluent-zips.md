## Washington Market Characteristics

### Tech Wealth Considerations
- RSU (Restricted Stock Unit) income
- Vesting schedules affect qualification
- Some lenders count 100%, others discount
- Wholesale brokers know which lenders work

### Jumbo Loan Limits (2026)
- King County: $1,149,825 (high-cost)
- Snohomish County: $1,149,825 (high-cost)
- Pierce County: $766,550 (standard)

### Common Borrower Profiles

**Tech Employees (Microsoft, Amazon, Google)**
- High W-2 income
- Significant RSU grants
- Need lenders who understand tech comp

**Self-Employed Tech Consultants**
- Bank statement programs
- 1099 income documentation
- Variable income patterns

**Investors**
- Seattle rental market active
- DSCR loans popular
- Portfolio building

**Foreign Nationals**
- Tech visa holders (H1B)
- International investors
- ITIN borrowers
