## Executive Summary

This design establishes MoTheBroker.com as the definitive authority on wholesale mortgage brokering in California through a comprehensive pillar/cluster content architecture, affluent zip code targeting, and educational authority positioning.

### Positioning Statement

**"The Wholesale Insider" + "The Local Wholesale Expert"**

- Educate borrowers on how wholesale pricing works and why retail is more expensive
- Dominate local markets (Orange County → California → Washington) with geo-specific content
- Target affluent zip codes where jumbo loans create the biggest broker advantage
