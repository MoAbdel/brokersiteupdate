## Key Messaging by Price Tier

### Ultra-Luxury ($3M+)
- Super jumbo loans up to $10M+
- Asset depletion for high net worth
- Discretion and privacy emphasis
- White-glove service positioning

### Luxury ($2M-$3M)
- Jumbo loans up to $5M
- Bank statement for business owners
- Investment property programs
- Multiple property financing

### Upper-Affluent ($1.5M-$2M)
- Standard jumbo programs
- Self-employed solutions
- Rate shopping advantage
- Faster closing capability

### Affluent/Threshold ($1M-$1.5M)
- Jumbo vs conforming education
- First-time jumbo buyer focus
- Rate comparison with banks
- Program flexibility emphasis
